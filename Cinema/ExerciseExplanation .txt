A cinema has availabe one theater which performs daily 2 screenings of one movie.
The capacity of each theater is 80 seats and the ticket costs 7 euros. This program:
For every customer:
  -reads his name, the number of the screening and the amound of tickets he wants to buy.
  -It checks if the amound of tickets is available, if yes it prints the cost,if not it prints a message with the available ticket number.
 the above process ends when you type "CLOSED" or both of the theater's tickets are sold out.
 After the ticket bookings:
  it prints the name of the customer to bought the most tickets.
  it prints the cinema's income.
  prints the name of the last customer who bought a ticket.
