A hotel is charging for room renting depending on the days of staying and the period of renting depending on the following array:

Days of Staying   Low Period     High Period
      1-3         40€/per day    70€/per day
      4-7         30€/per day    55€/per day
      >7          25€/per day    50€/per day

This program for each of 500 hotel reservation during the prevous year:
-is reading from keyboard the days and the period (HIGH or LOW) the customer is renting.
-it calls the function charge which is calculating according to the array above, the charging.
-It prints the sum of each period for the previous year.
